ERIC BARONE

Creator of Stardew Valley

We don’t think of popular video games—the kinds that sell millions of copies—as peaceful or intimate. But with the beloved Stardew Valley, Eric Barone discovered the alchemy of quiet gamemaking.

Here is Barone's Journey :

  • 1987 - Born in Los Angeles, California, U.S.
  • 2011 - Graduated from University of Washington Tacoma with a degree in computer science
  • 2012 - Barone began working on Stardew Valley. He created the game independently, as its sole designer, programmer, animator, artist, composer, and writer. To complete the game, Barone worked 12 hours a day, seven days a week, for four and a half years
  • 2016 - Released the game, Stardew Valley
  • 2017 - He was named by Forbes magazine in their list "30 Under 30: Games" due to his work on Stardew Valley
  • 2019 - Barone has been assisted on Stardew Valley by another designer.
  • 2020 - Barone announced that he was working on several new games, with one of them set in the Stardew Valley universe
  • 2021 - Barone and board game designer Cole Meideros released a cooperative board game adaptation of Stardew Valley.
  • 2022 - Barone initially released Stardew on the PC before later developing it for other consoles. He had sold over 20 million copies.
